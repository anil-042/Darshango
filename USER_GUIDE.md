## 2. Dashboard Overview
Upon logging in, you are greeted with the **Main Dashboard**.
- **KPI Cards**: View high-level metrics such as Total Projects, Active Agencies, Total Funds Utilized, and Pending Inspections.
- **Project Map**: A geographical representation of project locations (if enabled).
- **Recent Alerts**: Real-time notifications about critical updates, delays, or fund discrepancies.
- **Quick Actions**: Shortcuts to create new projects or add agencies.

---

## 3. Project Management
The core of the system is managing the lifecycle of projects under PM-AJAY.

### View Projects
- Navigate to **"Projects"** in the sidebar.
- View a list of all projects with status (Ongoing, Completed, Delayed), budget, and location.
- Use filters to search by state, district, or status.

### Create a New Project
1. Click the **"+ New Project"** button.
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Basic Info**: Name, Location (State/District), Sector.
   - **Budget**: Estimated cost and sanctioned amount.
   - **Timeline**: Start date and expected completion date.
   - **Agency**: Assign an implementing agency.
3. Click **"Create Project"**.

### Project Details & Tracking
Click on any project ID to view its detailed dashboard:
- **Overview**: Progress bar, financial summary, and key dates.
- **Milestones**: Track specific phases (e.g., "Foundation Laid", "50% Completed"). Update status as milestones are achieved.
- **Documents**: Upload and manage MOUs, approvals, and technical sanctions.
- **Messages**: Internal communication specific to this project.

---

## 4. Agency Management
Manage the implementing agencies (NGOs, Government bodies) responsible for executing projects.

### Agency Mapping
- Navigate to **"Agency Mapping"**.
- View a list of registered agencies and their performance ratings.

### Onboard an Agency
1. Click **"Add Agency"**.
2. Enter details: Name, Registration Number, Contact Info, and Type.
3. The agency is now available to be assigned to projects.

---

## 5. Fund Flow Tracking
Monitor the flow of funds from the central ministry to the implementing agencies.

### Global Fund View
- Navigate to **"Fund Flow"**.
- **Sankey Diagram**: Visualize the flow of money across different levels (Ministry -> State -> District -> Agency).
- **Transaction History**: View a ledger of all disbursements and utilizations.

### Project Fund Tracking
- Within a Project's detail view, go to the **"Funds"** tab.
- Record new disbursements or utilization certificates (UCs).
- The system automatically calculates the utilization percentage.

---

## 6. Inspection & Monitoring
Ensure quality and progress through physical inspections.

### Inspection Reports
- Navigate to **"Inspections"**.
- View scheduled and completed inspections.

### Submit a Report
1. Select a project.
2. Click **"Add Inspection"**.
3. Enter details: Date, Inspecting Officer, Observations, and Status (Satisfactory/Critical).
4. **Upload Images**: Attach site photos as proof of progress.

---

## 7. Communication & Support

### AI Assistant (Help Chatbox)
- Click the **"Need Help?"** button in the bottom-left corner.
- **Ask Questions**: Type queries like "How do I add a project?" or "Show fund details".
- The AI assistant (integrated with n8n) will provide instant guidance and navigation help.

### Alerts & Notifications
- Check the **Alerts Feed** on the dashboard for automated system warnings (e.g., "Budget exceeded for Project X", "Inspection overdue").

---

## 8. User Management (Admin Only)
- Navigate to **"Users"** (if visible/authorized).
- Create accounts for other officials, project managers, or agency representatives.
- Assign roles (Admin, Manager, Viewer) to control access permissions.

---

### Troubleshooting
- **Login Issues**: Ensure credentials are correct. Contact system admin for password resets.
- **Data Not Loading**: Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, use the "Need Help?" chatbox to report it.
